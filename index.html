About Me

An animator by trade, I specialize in motion graphics for publicity, doing mostly freelance work in the industry, sometimes working in tandem with my wife Ariana.

I've found myself branching into new areas of interest, one being web design and a full-stack developer, whilst studying a master's degree in existential psychotherapy.
I thoroughly enjoy trying new things and take myself as a quick learner (although many times I forget things just as fast). My personal interests are animation, board games, and tabletop games.
