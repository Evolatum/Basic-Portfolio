About Me

An animator by trade, I specialize in motion graphics for publicity and full-stack web development, doing mostly freelance work. I tend to work in tandem with my wife Ariana (who would probably comment that I work in tandem with her), and draw inspiration from all sources, most of all entertainment.

I've worked in a wide variety of jobs, making animated sports content for a television network broadcasting in the USA, teaching and aiding seniors with cognitive and computer skills, and creating ads for political campigns; I like to think of myself and my work as versatile.

I thoroughly enjoy trying new things and take myself as a quick learner (although many times I forget things just as fast). My personal interests are all kinds of animation, board and tabletop games, and world-building. I'm currently working toward a master's degree in existential therapy.
